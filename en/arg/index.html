Absolute reading group

The goal of this reading group is to understand the classification of fields having the same Absolute Galois group as Qp. It might be a bit ambitious. But I think it's probably possible to at least prove that having this absolute Galois group implies being of characteristic 0 and admitting a henselian valuation with minimum positive element and residue field Fp. We will see.

Schedule

We meet on Thursdays at 14:00 (probably in the seminar room). First meeting on 16.04 to discuss organization.
  • Talk 0. Various antipasti: Artin-Schreier's results (see 3.21 to 3.27 in GKS for why it is useful to introduce it), model theoretic notions such as elemetary equivalences, complete theories etc. Mention that "small" Galois group are preserved under elementary equivalences (GKS lemma 4.27). If time, present the outline of the main proof of GKS, that is, the contents of section 5.
  • Talks 1&2. Valuation theory: most of the contents of GKS section 2.
  • Talks 3&4. Tapping the Galois group: recovering as much infos on the field from its Galois group. most of the contents of GKS section 3.
  • Talk 5. Transfer. GKS section 4. One method of transfer is enough, and then the proof can be wrapped up and finished. But given time, it would really be nice to go look at the perfectoid stuff and Jahnke-Kartas.
